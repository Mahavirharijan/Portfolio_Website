About Me

Professional Summary

I am a cybersecurity student with practical experience in penetration testing, vulnerability assessment, and web application security. I have worked on real-world simulated projects involving reconnaissance, scanning, and vulnerability identification using tools such as Kali Linux, Nmap, Nikto, Hydra, Shodan, and Wappalyzer. I enjoy analyzing security flaws, preparing detailed reports, and learning advanced offensive security techniques to grow as a professional cybersecurity analyst.

Career Objective

To pursue a career in cybersecurity with a strong focus on penetration testing, vulnerability assessment, and ethical hacking. I aim to apply my hands-on experience, technical skills, and continuous learning mindset to identify security weaknesses and help organizations strengthen their digital security posture.
